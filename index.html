Frequently Asked Questions

What type of airplanes will you be making?

We like to focus on modern aircraft, with a focus on military aviation. One of the largest deciding factors on if we can work on an aircraft is the amount of reference material we can obtain. Check out Discord for more information on current and future projects, or if you have a potential project for us.

What type of realism can we expect?

Although we don't like to call sim aircraft "study level", we aim to create our aircraft to the best of our abilities and knoledge. Much like other freeware organizations, we aim to continually improve our products over time. If you are interested in working with us, please contact any of our administration on our Discord server or via email.

When will the next update be?

There are NO release dates or timelines for freeware projects. These projects take a sideline to our real lives, and are heavily dependent on how much time we are capable of investing into this project. When we are ready for the next update, we will post it on our Discord server.
